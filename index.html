The Mission

Aud-e-Prac Incorporated is a well established firm of Chartered Accountants (SA) and Registered Auditors. We value quality, integrity, commitment, client service, trust and respect.

"Always give people more than what they expect to get"
~ Nelson Boswell

With offices in Vanderbijlpark, Vereeniging and Wolmaransstad, We have over 25 years of experience and serve a broad spectrum of clients in the Vaal Triangle as well as other parts of South Africa.

We are a member of

IRBA

The Independant Regulatory Board for Auditors helps us ensure that we protect the financial interest of the South African public and international investors in South Africathrough the effective regulation of audits conducted by registered auditors, in accordance with internationally recognised standards and processes.

SAICA

The South African Institute of Chartered Accountants (SAICA) is the foremost accountancy body in South Africa and one of the leading Institutes in the world. It plays an influential role in a highly dynamic business sector, and it recognizes us as a member because we follow similar principles in our services.
